The Victorian Era

The Victorian Era took place during the mid 19th century to early 20th century, in which Queen Victoria ruled over the United Kingdom. During this period there were wide religous reforms, social reforms, and governmental reforms across the world. With this period of course came new popular styles in hats.

As the Catholic Church slowly lost power, along with feudalism starting to fall, came a new era characterized by the rise of the middle class and professional society with hat styles becoming popular like top hats, bowler hats, fedoras, halo hats, boater hats, sailor hats, homburg hats, as well as ribbons and hairpieces becoming popular.
