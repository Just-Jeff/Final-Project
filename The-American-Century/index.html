The American Century

The American Century took place during the 20th century, in which the United States rose to power through trade, and the victories of World War 1 and 2.

This period is characterized by the growth and influence of the United States, both economically and politically across the world. During this period hat styles changed greatly to reflect the culture of the hard working class in both urban areas and rural areas.

Some of these hats that became a staple of this era were cowboy hats (known as stetson hats), newspaper caps, baseball hats, porkpie hats, campaign hats, garrison caps, hard hats, and peaked caps.
