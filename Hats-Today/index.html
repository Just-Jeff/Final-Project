Hats Today

Here in the 21st century, we see hats not only based on previous time periods like the American Century and the Victorian Age, but also hats that reference to earlier periods with straw hats and felt caps to the Middle Ages and some of the first hats.

Our style and culture of hats have been building upon each other to finally lead us to where we are today. One vast difference between hats of today and hats previously, is that hats are no longer just used for function or to show social status, they're also used for marketing.

Below it a series of collected data from the 2016 Global Advertising Specialities Impressions Study, which found some statisitcal trends among modern day hat wearers in the US, and the power of marketing that hats can bring with them.
